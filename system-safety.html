System Safety

System safety is a critical aspect of DO-178C. It involves identifying and mitigating hazards that could lead to accidents or incidents.

The system safety process includes the following activities:

  • Hazard analysis: Identifying potential hazards that could lead to accidents or incidents.
  • Risk assessment: Assessing the likelihood and severity of each hazard.
  • Safety requirements: Developing requirements to mitigate the identified hazards.
  • Verification: Verifying that the safety requirements have been met.

System safety is an iterative process. The activities are performed throughout the software development lifecycle, and the results of each activity are used to improve the safety of the system.
