Welcome to the DO-178C Blog

This website is dedicated to providing information and resources related to DO-178C, a standard for software development in airborne systems. DO-178C is a standard for the development of safety-critical airborne systems. This blog will cover the key aspects of DO-178C, including development assurance, system safety, intended function, and DO-178C supplements.
